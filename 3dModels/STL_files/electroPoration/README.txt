# Summary

Cuvette holder arm for standard GenePulse electroporation units.  Holds standard ~1cm x ~1cm electroporation cuvettes, and clicks perfectly into the rest of the GenePulse unit to permeabilize cells and facilitate DNA transformation.

# Print Settings

Printer Brand: LulzBot
Printer: TAZ 5
Rafts: Doesn't Matter
Supports: Yes
Infill: 20%

Notes: 
This was printed on a LulzBot Taz5 printer, with a 0.35 mm extruder nozzle.  I've only used ABS, but it's probably good with HIPS or PLA too.  Likely doesn't need a brim or anything, but I do recommend supports depending on the orientation on the print bed.
